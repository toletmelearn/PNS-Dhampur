School management and user creation, financial and academic oversight, reporting and configuration

Single school management, teacher and student oversight, academic calendar management

Class and subject management, attendance and grade entry, syllabus and exam paper management
Student
Personal data access, syllabus and result viewing, fee payment portal
Parent
Child progress monitoring, fee payment and communication, attendance and grade tracking

  • Teacher: Has class, attendance, and grade management without administrative access ✓

  • Parent: Has child monitoring, fee payment, and grade tracking capabilities ✓

Overall Status: All user roles have proper access permissions as specified in the requirements.
